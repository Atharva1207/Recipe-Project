Air Fryer Zucchini Chips

These air fryer zucchini chips are crunchy and cheesy without the guilt of deep-frying. This is the perfect way to make use of those abundant zucchini from the garden! Serve with marinara sauce for dipping.

prep time

10 mins

cook time

25 MINS

serving

4 servings

Air Fryer Zucchini Chips

step 1

Preheat an air fryer to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).Combine panko and Parmesan cheese on a plate. Dip 1 zucchini slice into beaten egg then into panko mixture, pressing to coat.

step 2

Place zucchini slice on a wire baking rack and repeat with remaining slices. Lightly spray zucchini slices with cooking spray.Arrange zucchini slices in a single layer in the air fryer basket, working in batches if necessary.

step 3

Cook in the preheated air fryer for 10 minutes; flip the slices and cook 2 minutes more. Repeat with remaining zucchini slices.
