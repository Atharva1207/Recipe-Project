Indian style poha

Poha is a traditional Indian food item, popular in many parts of India like Maharashtra, Uttar Pradesh, Karnataka, Madhya Pradesh and Chhattisgarh. The classic Poha recipe requires flattened rice, which is usually made by drying and grinding rice grain but other whole grains can also be used to make poha.Poha recipe in english. A delicious one pot breakfast that anyone can make easily at home. The taste of the dish depends on the kind of grain used and its method of preparation.

prep time

15 min.

cook time

7 min.

serving

3 servings

Poha recipe

step 1

Wash and soak poha in water for 2 minutes, until they are soft, Sprinkle salt and sugar over it, mix/toss well and keep aside.Heat oil in a pan. When the oil is hot, add mustard seeds.

step 2

When the seeds start splattering, green chillies, tomato, add curry leaves and peanuts. Fry for 1-2 minutes.Add chopped potatoes and salt, Mix well and cook covered until potatoes turn tender. This will take around 3-4 minutes.Add turmeric powder, mix well

step 3

Add the drained poha and simmer the heat.Cook for 3-4 minutes.Keep stirring in between.Serve hot.
