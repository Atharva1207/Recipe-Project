Vegetable Pulao Recipe

When you are short of time simply make this vegetable pulao and serve it with a raita of your choice.is a spicy rice dish prepared by cooking rice with various vegetables and spices. In this recipe, assortment of spices, veggies and rice are first sautéed in oil and ghee and then cooked in pressure cooker. However, the uniqueness of this easy mixed vegetable pulao

prep time

51-60 minutes

cook time

21-25 minutes

serving

4 servings

Vegetable Pulao Recipe

step 1

Heat ghee in a non-stick pan. Add cumin seeds, peppercorns, green cardamoms, cloves and bay leaves, mix and sauté for 30 seconds.Add cauliflower, French beans, carrots and green chillies and mix well.

step 2

Add green peas and mix well. Add rice and salt and mix lightly.

step 3

Add 3 cups water, mix and bring to a boil.Cover and cook on low heat till rice is done.Serve hot garnished with a coriander sprig.

ingredients

Cauliflower florets 8-10,French beans stringed and chopped 5-6,Carrots peeled and chopped 2 medium,Green peas 1/4 cup

Basmati rice soaked 1 1/2 cups,Ghee 1 tablespoon,Cumin seeds 1 tablespoon

Black peppercorns 4-5,Green cardamoms 3-4,Cloves 3-4

Bay leaves 2,Green chillies slit 2,Salt to taste
