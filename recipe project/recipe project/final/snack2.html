Chocolate Protein Balls

Easy protein ball recipe using chocolate protein powder, natural peanut butter, oats, and seeds. These energy balls are perfect for after-school snacking!

prep time

10 mins

cook time

30 MINS

serving

10 servings

Chocolate Protein Balls

step 1

Stir oats, peanut butter, honey, chocolate, flax seeds, chia seeds, and protein powder together in a bowl until evenly mixed.

step 2

Cover bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for 30 minutes.

step 3

Scoop chilled mixture into balls. Keep cold until serving.

ingredients

1 cup rolled oats,½ cup natural peanut butter,⅓ cup honey

¼ cup chopped dark chocolate

2 tablespoons flax seeds,2 tablespoons chia seeds

1 tablespoon chocolate-flavored protein powder, or to taste

Nutrition Info

carbohydrates 21.5g
